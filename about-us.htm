Incepted in 2001, Javelin Communications FZ LLC is a leading IT Consultant and integrated enterprise solution provider, headquartered in Dubai Internet City, UAE with business operations across MEANA (Middle East, Asia, North Africa) and Europe.
Javelin, best described as the most penetrating force in the arena of Enterprise Solutions, will deliver unparalleled business value to its global clients through a blend of process-excellence, unique proven methodology, world-class delivery model, decades of accumulated cross-domain experience and state-of-the-art infrastructure. Javelin’s best-of-the breed and innovative enterprise products are aimed for dynamic business environment where competitive business strategies and cutting-edge technologies converge.
Javelin’s Business Process services span Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP), Customer Relationship Management (CRM), Project Management, and Human Capital Management (HCM & Payroll), Document Management, e-Learning and e-governance etc. Beside, Javelin provides training and PM Competency Development.
By customizing our standard products, we define, design, develop and implement solutions that are contextual to the end-user. Our technology-driven industry-specific solutions will meet or exceed the unique requirements of our global customers, giving fantastic competitive edge over their business-rivals in the fierce market-competition, thereby increasing ROI, bolstering up sales and reducing TOC.
Our proven methodology has been honed and refined over multiple implementations. In addition, our world-class delivery model makes economic sense and helps provide cost-effective services.
